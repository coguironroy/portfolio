Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee shop sales for the selected period. It visualizes key metrics such as total sales, total orders, total quantity sold, sales trends, and product category performance. The dashboard is designed to offer insights into hourly and daily sales performance to identify peak hours patterns, sales performance by store locations, and best selling products.

DVD Rentals - Sales Performance

This DVD Rental Dashboard provides key insights into rental performance, revenue, and customer behavior. It helps analyze trends in the DVD rental business over time, offering a data-driven approach to decision-making.

Plants Business Sales Performance

This Power BI Performance Dashboard provides an overview of sales performance, highlighting key metrics such as Year-to-Date (YTD) sales, Prior Year-to-Date (PYTD) sales, and Gross Profit percentage. The dashboard includes visualizations such as a treemap for top-selling countries, a waterfall chart for sales changes by month, and bar and line charts for product and monthly trends. Key insights indicate a -3.9% sales decline compared to the previous year, with China contributing 31% of total sales.
